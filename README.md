## 👋 About Me

I’m a **dentist-turned-researcher** working at the intersection of **global health, artificial intelligence, and oral health equity**.

My work focuses on building and evaluating systems that are:

- **Equitable across populations**
- **Clinically meaningful**
- **Deployable in real-world health systems**

Currently at **St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital**, with training in **Epidemiology & Biostatistics at Johns Hopkins** and five years of clinical practice across India.

---

## 🌍 Global Health & Equity Research

Health innovation must work everywhere — not just where data is abundant.

I study:
- Implementation and evaluation in diverse health systems  
- Mixed-methods approaches in low-resource settings  
- Community-centered and equity-driven research design  
- Policy-relevant health systems analysis  

---

## 🧠 AI & Fairness in Clinical Care

Artificial intelligence in medicine must be accountable, transparent, and fair.

My research includes:
- Calibration and subgroup performance analysis  
- Bias assessment in radiology AI tools  
- Development of consensus-based fairness recommendations  
- Translating fairness metrics into clinical practice  

---

## 🦷 Oral Health & Data Justice

Oral disease is global — but diagnostic data is not.

I work on:
- Representation gaps in oral health datasets  
- Ethical development of AI-driven diagnostic tools  
- Equity-centered frameworks for global oral health research
